# Image Editing

Free and open-source tools for image editing and digital art creation.

## Comparison

| Tool | Focus | Platforms | Free | Notes |
|------|-------|-----------|------|-------|
| [GIMP](gimp.md) ⭐ 6.0k | Photo editing & compositing | macOS, Linux, Windows | Yes | The most feature-complete free Photoshop alternative |
| [Krita](krita.md) ⭐ 9.4k | Digital painting & illustration | macOS, Linux, Windows | Yes | Superior brush engine; aimed at artists rather than photo editors |

## Choosing Between Them

- Use **GIMP** for photo retouching, image compositing, and general-purpose raster editing.
- Use **Krita** for digital painting, concept art, illustration, and animation.
- Both tools can handle the other's use cases, but each excels in its primary domain.
