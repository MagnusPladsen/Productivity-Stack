# Modulz

> Visual code editor for designing, developing, documenting, and deploying design systems without writing code.

> **Status: Discontinued.** Modulz was acquired by WorkOS in June 2022 and shut down on July 1, 2022. The team's open-source work lives on through [Radix UI](https://www.radix-ui.com/) and [Radix Themes](https://www.radix-ui.com/themes), maintained by WorkOS.

**Platforms:** Web-based
